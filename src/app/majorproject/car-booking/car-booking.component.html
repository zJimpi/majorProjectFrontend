  • The car will be provided with a full tank of fuel at the time of renting. Users are responsible for refueling the car to the same level upon return; otherwise, charges will apply.

  • Users are responsible for any damage caused to the car during the rental period. They must report accidents immediately and adhere to the rental agreement's procedures.
  • Insurance coverage may be available and should be discussed with the rental service.
  • Prohibited activities include illegal use, racing, towing, off-road driving, or any activity that may damage the vehicle or violate the law.
  • Late returns may incur additional charges as per the rental service's policy.
  • Cancellation charges may apply based on the timing of the cancellation and the booking agreement.
  • The rental service is responsible for maintaining the vehicle in good working condition. Users should report any issues promptly.
  • Users agree to indemnify and hold harmless the rental service from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from their use of the rented vehicle.
