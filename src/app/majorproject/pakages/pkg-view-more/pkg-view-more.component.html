Additional Activities

Parasailing

Parasailing is a recreational activity where participants are towed behind a boat while attached to a parachute-like canopy, soaring above the water or land.

Kayaking

Kayaking involves navigating waterways using a small, narrow boat propelled by a double-bladed paddle, offering both leisurely exploration and exhilarating whitewater adventures.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling is an underwater activity that involves swimming on the water's surface with a diving mask and a snorkel, allowing participants to observe marine life and explore the ocean depths.

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is an underwater activity where divers use self-contained breathing apparatus to explore the depths of the ocean, encountering diverse marine life and captivating underwater landscapes.

Few Spots


Cellular Jail

Cellular Jail, located in the Andaman Islands, India, was a colonial-era prison infamous for its isolated cell structure, used by the British to incarcerate political prisoners during India's struggle for independence.

Neil Island

Neil Island is a picturesque island known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs, located in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands of India.

Ross Island

Ross Island is a small island in Antarctica known for its historic huts and scientific research stations.

Radhanagar Beach

Radhanagar Beach is a stunning white sand beach located on Havelock Island in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, known for its crystal-clear waters and breathtaking sunsets.
